Single Serve French Toast

1. In a flat bowl mix Together 1 egg, 1/2 cup milk, & 1/2 tsp cinnimon.

2. Dip one of your bread sliced and let it soak for a few minutes.

3. Once It's ready just put a little oil on the pan and cook it fliping half way through.

4. so both sides are cooked. While you cook this slice you can reapet the soaking process which each one.

5. Serve with...

Options

  • fruits,
  • cinnimon & sugar,
  • Maple Syrup,
  • Honey,
  • or whatever you like,

6. Enjoy!
